INDEPENDENCE DAY

Every year on August 15, India celebrates Independence Day, transforming the country into a painting filled with the colours of pride and patriotism. On this date in 1947, the nation declared its independence from the 200-year British rule. This historic occasion honours the nation's laborious transition from the confines of colonial control to the lofty heights of sovereignty.
